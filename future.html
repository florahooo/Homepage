The Future

CS50 Reflection

CS50 definitely opened my eyes towards all things computer science. Before, I didn’t even know what a data structure was, or what command line arguments were or even how IP addresses work! CS50 definitely made me a more appreciative person, just knowing how much work went into creating some of the most spectacular pieces of technology we see today.

The course so far has definitely not been easy for me, and I had many frustrating nights trying to understand many of the topics. But, I’m definitely thankful for what CS50 gave me, not only the knowledge but also the mindset. I learned how to approach problems, and how to be (very) patient when things aren’t going your way. I learned to not cry or burst into anger when nothing seems to work, and finally, I learned to talk to a rubber duck when you’re in dire situations. Even if I don’t go into programming/computer science in the future, the mindset that I left with is definitely one of my most valuable things I’ve ever taken from a course.

So...What am I doing after CS50?

Technically...I’m not done the course yet (whoopsies!), I still have many many problems to do, but after CS50, I’m hoping to apply my knowledge and continue growing from the ladder CS50 set me up with. I’ve found that I really enjoy web development! It’s super fun to see your designs come to life, and javascript is great (if you can execute it correctly ;-;). After this, I wanna continue learning, and hopefully learn a web framework such as React, Angular or Vue. I may also take CS50 for web development we’ll seeeeeeee.

I really want to create a full-fledged web application with a full on user login system using front-end, back-end and databases. My current idea is to build a full-fledged productivity app, that includes a lot of the most popular productivity apps into one. I also just want to get better at all the languages I currently know. I’ll definitely be sharpening up my python skills (I don’t know about C tho, pretty sure its a lost cause) and I’ll continue creating programs that make me happy, or are just for fun!
